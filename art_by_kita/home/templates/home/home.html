Art by Kita

Originally from Belfast and now based in Co. Clare, I specialise in creating stunning landscapes and dreamscapes using acrylic on pressed canvas.

With a deep passion for capturing nature's beauty and surreal visions, each painting is crafted with care and imagination.

In addition to my collection of unique works, I am more than happy to bring your ideas to life, whether through photos or custom requests.

As a Neurodivergent artist with Autism and ADHD, my creative process is shaped by a unique perspective, infusing every piece with emotion and depth.
